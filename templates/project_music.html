My Music – JoberSynth.exe

JoberSynth.exe is my music project: emotional, atmospheric tracks that blend electronic textures, trip-hop and downtempo vibes. The idea is to create soundtracks for my own universe (Sol, Nova City, NovaReels) and use music as a core part of the experience.

Style & influences

  • Downtempo, trip-hop, ambient electronic.
  • Cinematic, emotional and spacey textures.
  • Designed to work as background for visuals, reels and storytelling.

Tools & workflow

  • DAW: FL Studio
  • Instruments: MIDI keyboard / controller
  • Sound design: VST synths, pads, textured drums
  • Integration: Use tracks inside NovaReels and Sol projects.

How it connects to my other projects

  • Music for AI reels generated with NovaReels.
  • Soundtracks for scenes and lore involving Sol and Nova City.
  • Background music for future videos, demos and portfolio content.

Status

Ongoing. I’m composing new tracks, cleaning old ideas and planning how to publish them (reels, YouTube, streaming platforms) as part of my broader creative ecosystem.
